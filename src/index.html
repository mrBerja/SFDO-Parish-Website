DISCOVER OUR PARISH LIFE

START LIVING LIFE

In the picturesque town of San Fernando, a thriving community has embraced the importance of living a purposeful life. The townspeople, influenced by their rich religious faith, have dedicated themselves to nurturing their spiritual, intellectual, and emotional growth, ultimately transforming their community into a harmonious and flourishing haven.

The parishioners, steeped in the teachings of Jesus Christ and the Sacred Scriptures, have learned to cultivate gratitude, forgiveness, and unwavering faith in the face of adversity. This collective sense of spiritual fulfillment has served as the cornerstone for the community's unwavering resilience and enduring love for one another.

About Us

St. John The Baptist Parish, situated in San Fernando, Camarines Sur, is a vibrant and spirited Catholic community, deeply rooted in the rich tradition of Filipino spirituality. Named after the illustrious biblical figure, St. John The Baptist Parish serves as a testament to the steadfast faith and devotion of its parishioners.
